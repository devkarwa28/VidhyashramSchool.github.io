Academics...

The primary objective of Vidhyashram is to impart scientific and progressive education with emphasis on inculcating and promoting Indian Culture & Tradition.


For excellence in academics, a highly enriched and innovative curriculum has been designed through which Vidhyashram is able to develop cognitive, socio-emotional and motor skills for junior students and for senior students it adheres to CBSE norms which creates inquisitiveness and awareness of students towards learning modules.
